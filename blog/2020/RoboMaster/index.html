What is MOBA?

What I like most about the competition is the introduce of the MOBA style confrontation. MOBA stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena and is originally a format of strategy video games. Famous MOBA games include League of Legends (LoL), Defense of The Ancients 2 (Dota 2) which is on the PC end and Honor of Kings on the mobile end. Normally five players work together as a team to combat another five player team. The victory condition is to destory the base of the enemy. To form a dominant fighting force, one team should be composed of units performing different roles. For instance, some units may focuse on the abilities of damage output and some may have high health points. Players should make good use of the units advantages and protect the weakness of their teammates to optimize the teamwork.
